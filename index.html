Snow Rider 3D

Snow Rider 3D is an adrenaline-pumping winter sports game that puts you on the most thrilling slopes. Carve through fresh powder, pull off incredible tricks, and race against time or friends in this ultimate snowboarding experience!

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

Master the Slopes

Navigate challenging terrain, dodge obstacles, and collect power-ups while maintaining top speed. Perfect your timing to execute jaw-dropping backflips, 360s, and grabs that'll earn you massive points.

Multiple Game Modes

Take on time trials, freestyle challenges, or compete in tournament mode. Race against friends in split-screen multiplayer or challenge global leaderboards in solo mode.

Features

  • Stunning 3D graphics with realistic snow physics
  • Multiple challenging tracks and environments
  • Solo and multiplayer game modes
  • Trick system with combo multipliers
  • Global leaderboards
  • Various characters and boards to unlock

Controls

  • Arrow keys or A/D to steer
